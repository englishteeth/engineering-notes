# Engineering Fieldnotes

Practical material from 30 years of designing, building, and occasionally inheriting software systems.

Not tutorials. Not hot takes. Just the things I've found worth writing down — because I've seen the same problems enough times to think a structured prompt is more useful than another opinion piece.

Most of it is aimed at engineers and architects working on systems that have grown beyond their original design. That's where the interesting problems tend to live.
